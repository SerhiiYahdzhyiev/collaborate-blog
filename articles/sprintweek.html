GISMA Skills Sprint Week

by Harsh Dani

Introduction:

GISMA University recently organized an exciting event called the Skills Sprint Week. This week-long program aimed to provide students with a unique opportunity to enhance their skills in a fast-paced, hands-on learning environment. Through a series of company visits, workshops and guest lectures, students were able to build valuable connections, gain valuable insights and develop essential skills for academic and professional journeys.

Visit to the Bundestag:

Skills Sprint Week kicked off with a visit to the Bundestag in Berlin, which was a workshop called “Beyond the Borders: Germany's Economic and Sustainable Influence in International Relations.” We were joined by Mr. Knut Abraham, a former Diplomat and a Member of the German Parliament. We dived deep into topics like the impact of war on Germany’s economy, NATO, future plans and some advice on integrating and growing in Germany.

Company visit to SAP:

Next up was a company visit to SAP at Potsdam. Students were given a tour around the building showing their brilliant facility. It was then followed by a presentation on SAP's entire journey to what its future projects and goals are. Students were taken in depth into few of the hot topics of the digital industry like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML) and Cloud based software. We were also shown the opportunities offered by SAP like some educational learning programs and pathways for students.

Open Talks:

Skills Sprint Week featured open talks that provided insights into topics like “What is Happening in FAANG?” by a Data Scientist at Facebook, “Startup companies in Berlin and their challenges” by an HR/Operation Manager at iomoto by ZF Car eWallet GmbH, “IT Integration and Automatization in Supply Chain Management” by a Global Inventory Manager at Wolt and a Product Manager at Tom Tom, all of whom shared their experiences with the students. These sessions provided invaluable insights into current industry trends, emerging technologies, career pathways as well as an opportunity to ask questions and interact with them.

Conclusion:

Overall it proved to be a transformative experience for all the students that participated. It provided experiences, skills and industry insights that will serve well in academic and professional endeavors by empowering practical skills and a forward-thinking mindset, GISMA University continues to cultivate the innovators of tomorrow.
